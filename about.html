About.

More About Web-devloping .

Web developers have a logical, scientific mindset when it comes to the world. Instead of seeking for a recognized landmark to help them if they were lost, they would glance up at the stars or drop a GPS pin.

Web developers also have a proclivity towards viewing the world as a big collection of data that can be exploited for a variety of purposes. If you asked a web developer to write a word in the center of a page, they'd look for the quickest, most efficient way to measure it, such as folding the paper into four squares evenly or using a ruler.

Web developers prefer to approach large projects one step at a time, paying close attention to the minutiae. If a web developer were developing a theme park, she'd spend her time delving into the physics of the rides and the maximum amount of people the park could handle, one small step at a time.
